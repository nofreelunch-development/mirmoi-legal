# Privacy Policy — Mirmoi
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-14  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-14

Mirmoi (“we”, “our”, “us”) is developed and operated 
by **nofreelunchdevs.com** (“Developer”).  
**Contact:** support@mirmoi.com

## What we collect
- **Support communications** (if you email us)
- **Advertising data (AdMob)** (if ads are enabled): device/app identifiers, ad impressions/clicks, anti-fraud signals
- **Purchase/subscription data (Google Play / Apple)**: product IDs and purchase tokens/receipts to unlock features
- **Diagnostics (Crashlytics)**: crash logs/stack traces/device/app context to improve stability
- **On-device settings**: favorites/liked quotes and preferences stored locally

## What we do NOT collect
We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, SMS/call logs, or health/financial credentials.

## How we use data
- App functionality and entitlements
- Show ads to free users and measure ad performance
- Restore purchases and prevent fraud
- Improve reliability and respond to support requests

## Sharing
We use third-party services:
- Google AdMob (ads/measurement)
- Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)
- Google Play Billing / Apple IAP (payments/subscriptions)
- Backend providers (if enabled for entitlements sync)

## Your choices
- Manage/cancel subscriptions in your app store settings
- Request deletion of backend data: email **support@mirmoi.com** with subject “Data Deletion Request”

## Children
Not directed to children under 13.

## Security
We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
We do not use custom encryption.

## Changes
We may update this policy and will update the date above.
